Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Blockchain Foundation Africa collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Blockchain Foundation Africa's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Blockchain Foundation Africa's visitors use its website. From time to time, Blockchain Foundation Africa may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Blockchain Foundation Africa also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on BCFAfrica blog posts. BCFAfrica only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to Blockchain Foundation Africa's websites choose to interact with Blockchain Foundation Africa in ways that require Blockchain Foundation Africa to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Blockchain Foundation Africa gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up for a blog at https://bcfafrica.com to provide a username and email address.

Security

The security of your Personal Information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Advertisements

Ads appearing on our website may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Blockchain Foundation Africa and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

Our Service may contain links to external sites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy and terms and conditions of every site you visit.

Aggregated Statistics

Blockchain Foundation Africa may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its website. Blockchain Foundation Africa may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Blockchain Foundation Africa does not disclose your personally-identifying information.

Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, Blockchain Foundation Africa uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Blockchain Foundation Africa uses cookies to help Blockchain Foundation Africa identify and track visitors, their usage of https://bcfafrica.com, and their website access preferences. Blockchain Foundation Africa visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Blockchain Foundation Africa's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Blockchain Foundation Africa's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Blockchain Foundation Africa's use of cookies.
